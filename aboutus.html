About Us

The people behind the name

Our Team

Here at ExtraBoard, we are dedicated to bringing you the best quality board and video games from across Ireland. It all started ten years ago, when Alan and Mick were trying to find quality games at reasonable prices that were locally sourced in Ireland. ExtraBoard was born. Now a team of six, the ExtraBoard family work around the clock to ensure that you're never bored. Old and young alike, there's something for everyone here at ExtraBoard. Sports? we got it! RPG? We got it! Farming Simulators? You guessed it, we got it! Join us on our journey to bring affordable games to Ireland, so everyone can experience the joy of games!
